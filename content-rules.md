## 1. Ownership & Permissions

* All submitted content must be **original work** created by you.
* If you use assets made by others, you must have **explicit written permission** from the original creator to use and share them.
* Third-party content must include **proper credit** and comply with its original license terms.

  * Use, modify, and distribute your content within the project.
  * Include it in the **server resource pack**.
  * Display it on **official media**, such as the website, screenshots, or promotional materials.
* You **retain full ownership** of your content. This rule simply allows CrayonSMP to use it within the scope of the project.

## 3. Content Restrictions

* Submissions must **not** contain or depict:

  * NSFW or sexual material
  * Discriminatory, hateful, or politically extremist content
  * Copyrighted or illegal materials
* Any form of hate symbols (e.g., Nazi imagery) or harassment-related content is strictly prohibited.
* All submissions must be **appropriate for all ages** and comply with **youth protection laws**.
  
## 4. Gameplay Balance

* All items, blocks, and mechanics must be **survival-friendly and balanced**.
  Examples:

  * Items should not deal excessive damage or provide unfair advantages.
  * Strength, durability, and abilities should be proportional to how difficult the item is to obtain.
* Content intended solely for creative or showcase purposes should be clearly labeled as such.

## 5. Visual & Artistic Style

* Custom designs should **follow the official Minecraft Style Guide** to ensure visual consistency.

## 6. Technical & Quality Standards

* All submissions must:

  * Function correctly without missing models, textures, or corrupted files.
  * Use **clear and unique file names** to avoid conflicts.
  * Keep texture sizes reasonable (preferably 16x or 32x).
* Content that causes crashes, lag, or compatibility issues may be rejected or removed.

## 7. Review & Enforcement

* All submissions are **subject to review** by the CrayonSMP staff team.
* The team reserves the right to:

  * Modify content to maintain consistency and performance.
  * Remove any submission that violates these rules or degrades server experience.
* Repeated or intentional violations may lead to temporary or permanent removal from the content submission program.
